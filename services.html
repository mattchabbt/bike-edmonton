Facility Bookings

Our downtown shop can be booked for suitable events, and have been configured for lectures, movie screenings, and dance parties. Rates vary depending on the nature of event and how much setup support you need.

Lunch & Learn Presentation

Book us for a 45-60 minute lunch-and-learn presentation for your office or group. We'll introduce key information for individuals interested in riding, including gear, on-road safety and basic mechanical checks.

Rent a Mechanic

Book our certified bike mechanics! For availability and pricing, and to tailor our services to your needs, please contact us

Bike Generator Rental

To book a single bike generator for your class or event, please contact Jan Przysiezniak. This generator can power a blender (smoothies!), light bulb, and other electric devices.

Rent a Mechanic

Buy a bike rack for your business or apartment building through Bike Edmonton. We order in bulk and pass the savings on to you.

We may not be able to offer rack sales & rentals at this time. Please contact us (see below) and we will let you know what we can provide at present.
