New At This?

Don't know anything about bicycle repairs?

No problem! Volunteer mechanics are on duty to assist you, from finding a bike that's right for you, adjusting your seat, or fixing a flat, right up to building an entire bike from just parts.

Payment Methods

We accept Cash, Debit, Visa, MasterCard, American Express and most common credit cards. No minimum payment is required.

General Info

You can learn how to fix your bicycle

You don't need to know anything about bike repair: our volunteer mechanics will teach you.

You can donate used bikes or parts.

You can buy refurbished used bicycles

You can buy used bike parts and a limited selection of new parts as well.

We do not allow DIY bleeding of hydraulics in our shop.

Our ability to service some modern or higher-end components, as well as suspension, is limited

We do not allow gas powered bikes in our shop.

We generally will not check stock of bikes & parts over the phone or email

You cannot store your bike at our shop and we cannot place items on hold.

We operate the oldest community bike shop program in North America.
